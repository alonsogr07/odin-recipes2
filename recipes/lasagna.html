LASAGNA

Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling (a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan).

Ingredients

  • Meat: ½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef
  • Diced Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Asselble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
